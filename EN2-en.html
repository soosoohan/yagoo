Word baseball (medium)

Game Rules
  1. Guess the secret English word.
  2. You can choose a word length of 3, 4, or 5 letters.
  3. You can allow or disallow repeated letters.
  4. S (Strike): Number of letters in the correct position.
  5. B (Ball): Number of letters in the word but in a different position.
  6. O (Out): Number of letters not included in the secret word.
  7. The game ends when you guess the word correctly or use up all attempts.
